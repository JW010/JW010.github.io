My name is Janay.

I'm driven to push the boundaries of what's possible, especially when it comes to creating more inclusive, human-centered products, brands and campaigns within the industry.

Graphic Design

5+ years

As a passionate and creative designer, I specialize in transforming ideas into visually captivating designs that convey their distinctive value and message in a meaningful way. My proficiency in branding and identity, packaging, and print design, coupled with my mastery of Adobe Suite programs like Photoshop and Illustrator, allows me to bring depth and dimension to my designs, creating a lasting impact.

Branding

4+ Years

Collaborating closely with clients, I create strong, unified brands that reflect their unique identities and appeal to their audiences. My approach involves gaining a comprehensive understanding of the client's vision, values, and target audience. I bring brands to life through a variety of mediums, including social media, digital assets, web pages, photography, and motion graphics.

Creative Strategy

3+ Years

My enthusiasm for innovation drives me to think outside the box and devise creative strategies through in-depth research and brainstorming. I enjoy planning and executing projects that effectively communicate brand messages and achieve desired results. Whether it's rebranding or launching a product, I aspire to create memorable and impactful projects that set companies apart.

Content Production

2+ Years

As a student with experience working on sets filming videos using various digital cameras and iPhones, I have developed a passion for editing and post-production. Whether it's creating content for social media platforms like TikTok or Instagram or producing professional videos, I am committed to delivering high-quality results that meet the specific needs of each project. My time working with photographers and as a set assistant has given me a solid understanding of the creative process.

UI/UX

1+ Years

As a UI/UX designer, I bring a passion for problem-solving and a natural talent for designing intuitive and user-friendly experiences. My understanding of key components such as user research and analysis, information architecture, interaction design, visual design, and usability testing, helps me create successful and engaging user experiences. Although I am new to this field, I am dedicated to improving my skills and staying up-to-date with the latest design trends. I love the challenge of UI/UX design and the satisfaction that comes from seeing a project come to life.
