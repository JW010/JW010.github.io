Graphic Design

5+ years

As a graphic designer, I bring my passion and creativity to each project, working with clients to turn their ideas into visually stunning designs that communicate their unique value and message in a meaningful way. I am skilled in a range of design projects, including branding and identity, packaging, and print design, and I have honed my skills in both traditional and digital mediums through years of experience and a drive to always improve and learn more. With a mastery of Adobe Suite programs like Photoshop and Illustrator, I have the tools to bring depth and dimension to my designs, elevating them to the next level.

Branding

4+ Years

As a designer, I understand the importance of creating a strong, cohesive brand for a business or organization. I approach branding projects by first working closely with the client to understand their vision, values, and target audience. This allows me to develop a unique and effective brand that reflects the client's unique identity and resonates with their audience. I then use a variety of mediums, including social media, digital assets, webpages, photography, and motion graphics, to bring the brand to life. I believe that a strong brand is more than just a logo or color scheme; it's a holistic representation of the values, mission, and personality of the company. Effective branding helps to differentiate a company from its competitors and create a lasting impression on customers and stakeholders. I enjoy the challenge of working with clients to develop a unique and effective brand that reflects their vision and resonates with their audience.

Creative Strategy

3+ Years

I bring a unique perspective to each project by combining a deep understanding of market trends and consumer behavior with a passion for innovation. My approach to creative strategy involves thorough brainstorming and research to understand the target audience and identify opportunities for differentiation. I then plan and execute campaigns that effectively communicate the brand message and drive results. Whether it's rebranding a company or launching a new product, my goal is to create compelling, impactful campaigns that leave a lasting impression.

Content Production

2+ Years

Having worked on sets filming videos with digital cameras, DSLRs, and even iPhones. My expertise in editing and post-production allows me to turn raw footage into compelling and impactful content, delivered on time and within budget. UI/UX

1+ Years

Although I am new to this field, I am dedicated to improving my skills and staying up-to-date with the latest design trends. I love the challenge of UI/UX design and the satisfaction that comes from seeing a project come to life.
